Healthy Ice-Cream

Intro

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Berries 20-25
Apple 2
Ice cream 8 scoops

Step 1: Take 4 medium glasses.
Step 2: Place 1 scoop of Ice cream into each glass.
Step 3: Add berries in each glass.
Step 4: Chop 1 apple into small bits and add it to each glass.
Step 5: Add one more scoop of ice cream to it.
Step 6: Chop the 2nd apple into 8 pieces and garnish 2 pieces in each of the glasses.

Fruit Salad

Intro

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
Oranges 2 (Medium sized)
Mayonnaise 1/4 cup
Seedless Grapes 1-2 cups
Apples 2 (small sized & chopped)
Bananas 2 (small sized & sliced)
Walnuts 1/3 cup (chopped)
Cherries 1/4 cup
Raisins 1/4 cup

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix all the ingredients expect mayonnaise.
Step 2:Add mayonnaise after mixing well and then mix again nicely.
Step 3:Now you can serve them and garnish with whatever you like.

Bacon Pizza

Intro

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Refrigerated Pizza Crust 1
Olive Oil 2 tb spoons (divided)
Eggs 6
Water 2 tb spoons
Bacon Bits 3 ounces
Shredded Cheese 1 Cup

Step 1:Unroll crust into a greased 15-in. x 10-in. x 1-in. baking pan; flatten dough and build up edges slightly. Brush with 1 tablespoon oil. Prick dough thoroughly with a fork. Bake at 400Â° for 7-8 minutes or until lightly browned.
Step 2:Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk eggs and water. In a small skillet, heat remaining oil until hot. Add eggs; cook and stir over medium heat until completely set.
Step 3: Spoon eggs over crust. Sprinkle with bacon and cheeses. Bake 5-7 minutes longer or until cheese is melted. Yield: 8 slices.

Source:Taste of Home-Bacon Pizza
